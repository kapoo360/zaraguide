HOW TO DEAL WITH SHIPMENTS?


TO PREPARE FOR SHIPMENT :

1-TAKE RAILS AND SPREAD IT IN STOCKROOM FOR HANGING ITEMS.

2-MAKE TOWER OF CHECK-IN BOXES FOR FOLDED ITEMS.

3- OPEN BOXES AND PUT HANING ITEMS IN RAIL AND FOLDED ITEMS IIN THE TOWER.

4-REMOVE NYLON EXCEPT WHITE CLOTHES TO AVOID GETTING DIRTY.

5-MAKE SURE TO SEPARATE EVERY BOX TO AVOID ANY PROBLEMS IN PICKING BY BUNDLE LEVEL.

6-START PRICING HANGING ITEMS AND PICKING 25 DELIVERY ITEMS AND HOUSING THE HOUSING ITEMS.
